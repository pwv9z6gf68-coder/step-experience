# Mr. Adrian's STEP Experience
### Strategies to Empower People — Louisiana Works TANF Simulation

A browser-based arcade game that puts players in the shoes of a STEP participant.
Build your Family Success Agreement, log participation hours, play arcade quests,
survive life invasions, and face the Compliance Review boss fight.

---

## 🚀 Deploy to GitHub Pages (Free — 10 minutes)

### Step 1 — Create a GitHub account
Go to **github.com** → Sign Up (free). Remember your username.

### Step 2 — Create a new repository
1. Click the **+** button (top right) → **New repository**
2. Name it: `step-experience`
3. Set to **Public**
4. Click **Create repository**

### Step 3 — Upload the files
1. On your new repo page, click **uploading an existing file**
2. Drag ALL these files into the upload area:
   - `index.html`
   - `app.jsx`
   - `manifest.json`
   - `sw.js`
   - `icon-192.png`
   - `icon-512.png`
3. Scroll down → click **Commit changes**

### Step 4 — Enable GitHub Pages
1. Click **Settings** (top of your repo)
2. Left sidebar → **Pages**
3. Under "Source" → select **Deploy from a branch**
4. Branch: **main** | Folder: **/ (root)**
5. Click **Save**

### Step 5 — Your app is live!
After 1–2 minutes, your URL will be:
```
https://YOUR-USERNAME.github.io/step-experience
```

## 📱 Install as a Phone App (PWA)

**iPhone / iPad:**
1. Open the URL in **Safari**
2. Tap the Share button (box with arrow)
3. Tap **"Add to Home Screen"**
4. Tap **Add** — done! Full-screen icon on your home screen.

**Android:**
1. Open the URL in **Chrome**
2. Tap the 3-dot menu → **"Add to Home Screen"** or **"Install App"**
3. Tap **Install** — done!

The app works **offline** after first load (service worker caches everything).

## 🎮 Game Features
- **5 Participation Tracks**: Standard 20hr, Standard 30hr (children 6+), Incapacity, Family Crisis, Sanctions Gauntlet
- **FSA Builder**: Family Success Agreement with 11 goals and real action steps
- **Arcade Quests**: Lane-runner mini-game for each FSA goal
- **Life Invasions**: 8 real-life narrative interrupts (Walmart incident, wrong crowd, baby daddy drama...)
- **Compliance Boss Fight**: 3-wave document catch/dodge challenge
- **Community Service** as a valid work activity
